Varnam

Varnam is the most important piece in any recital and involves Jathi Korvais and Theermanams and a theme is elaborated with Abhinaya depicting the emotions of the diety portrayed. Mathe Varnom is a thematic representation on Mother Goddess Parvathy. The dance represents the power that runs the universe with specific praise for feminine empowerment and strength. The dance juxtaposes the Divine Mother and Her mortal counterpart in finely crafted wording. The item portrays Goddess in the gentle form of protection, love, and care & her ferocious form to destroy evil forces.

Today amruta is presenting an abridged version of Mathe Varnom.

Composer-Sri.Muthiah Bhagavathar

Ravanastotram

The Ravana Stotram, commonly known as the Shiv Tandav Stotram, is a powerful Sanskrit hymn dedicated to Lord Shiva. It was traditionally composed by the Asura King Ravana to praise Shiva's strength and cosmic dance. Chanting or listening to it is believed to remove negative energies and bring mental strength. This powerful mantra is supposed to be composed by the mighty king Ravana who was an ardent devotee of Lord Shiva.Ravana in his pride tried to lift Mount.kailasa.Scared Goddess parvathy ran to her husband lord Shiva.Shiva just pressed down his small toe on the floor and kailasa fell upon King Ravana.He realized his mistake and asked for forgiveness chanting this slokam.
